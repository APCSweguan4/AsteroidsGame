Asteroids

Controls
W: Accelerate
A: Turn Left
D: Turn Right
Shift: Hyperspace
Space: Shoot
1: Invincibility Barrier

Each large asteroid is worth 20 points while each small asteroid is worth 50 points. Every 300 points, there is an extra asteroid that is added to the number that spawn (up to a limit of 10). Every 1000 points, you get an extra life (up to a limit of 6).
